About

Dedicated to bringing the latest, unfiltered news to the community, this website aims to deliver everything MAPUA related. From announcements to main events, MAPUA's community members will find much of the essential reports that have happened, are happening, and will happen in the campus.

The Intention

The developers had in mind a more fleshed-out news site that's tailor made for anyone who wants to know the university's current affairs. Publishing statements made by the heads of the university, broadcasting necessary information to the community and announcing awards and ceremonies for those who've won in competitions and are academically accomplished.

The Inspiration

Us developers were inspired to make this website for the sake of convenience and collection. The website can act as both an updater for MAPUA's community and an archive. It can also act as a pool of responses and feedback for said news that the university can take notes of and make a few adjustments in the future for the betterment of the entire community.
