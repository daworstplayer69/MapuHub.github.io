Letran goes 2-0; Mapua, San Sebastian bounce back in MLBB Varsity Cup

Posted by By Bea Micaller, GMA News on November 11, 2021

The Colegio de San Juan de Letran remained undefeated in Day 2 of the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Varsity Cup on Thursday after outlasting the debuting Jose Rizal University.

The Intramuros-based squad swept JRU, 2-0, marking the second consecutive outing that the school won in a dominant fashion following a 2-0 victory over San Beda University on Wednesday.

JRU, which is bannered by JL Delos Santos, Jan Marc Abaoag, Hosea Mananquil, Dustin Aguado, and Donvic Aguado, opened their campaign with a 2-0 loss to fellow debutant University of Perpetual Help System Dalta earlier in the day.

Meanwhile, both the Mapua University and the San Sebastian College-Recoletos bounced back from their respective defeats on Day 1.

The Cardinals bested the De La Salle -College of Saint Benilde, 2-0, while the Stags escaped the Arellano University, 2-0.

The top eight schools after the qualifiers will advance to the playoff round where the top four are seeded to the upper bracket while the bottom four will battle it out in the lower bracket.

The semifinal round is tipped to be in a best-of-five format while the championship match will be played in a best-of-seven series.
