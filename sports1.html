Lyceum defeats Mapua to rule MLBB Varsity Cup

Posted by By Bea Micaller, GMA News on November 20, 2021

The Lyceum of the Philippines University (LPU) reigned supreme in the first-ever Collegiate Center for Esports (CCE) Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Varsity Cup after overthrowing Mapua University, 4-2, in their best-of-seven grand finals on Saturday.

Yancy "Momopesky" Remulla received the MVP citations after he recorded 6 kills, 1 death, and 6 assists in six games.

"This is important because even though it's not in basketball, we were still able to bring honor to our school. That's why we're happy with this championship in esports," said Remulla in the post-game interview.

Remulla, whose team had a spotless run en route to the title, said he also credited all their opponents for coming out and joining the tournament.

"We all enjoyed the games and we bonded although it's not inside the [basketball] court."
